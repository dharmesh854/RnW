## 📊 Probability Project Summary

🔹 We created a student dataset containing study hours, attendance, group discussion, previous test score, and final exam result.

🔹 We learned the basic concepts of Probability such as experiment, outcome, event, and sample space.

🔹 We calculated **Empirical Probability** using real dataset values.
📌 Example: Probability of students passing the exam.

🔹 We understood **Theoretical Probability** using assumptions instead of real data.

🔹 We created a **Probability Distribution Table** for students passing among 3 selected students.

🔹 We calculated the **Mean** and **Variance** of the random variable using probability formulas.

🔹 We used a **Venn Diagram** 🟢 to visualize:

* Students studying more than 10 hours
* Students having attendance above 80%
* Common students satisfying both conditions

🔹 We created a **Contingency Table** to compare:

* Group Discussion participation
* Final Exam Pass/Fail results

🔹 We calculated:
✅ Joint Probability
✅ Marginal Probability
✅ Conditional Probability

🔹 We understood that:
📌 Group discussion and passing exam are **dependent events**.

🔹 We applied **Bayes Theorem** 🧠 to find:
👉 Probability of passing when attendance is high.

🔹 Finally, the analysis showed that:
📈 Higher study hours, attendance, and group discussion participation improve the chances of passing exams.
